# Customer Retention & Revenue Analysis

> 📊 93.09% of revenue is driven by repeat customers, while the top 10% contribute 61.41% of total sales.

This project analyzes 541K+ real retail transactions to uncover:
- customer retention behavior  
- revenue concentration risk  
- high-value customer segments  

The goal is to translate raw transactional data into actionable business decisions.

---

## Key Findings

- Repeat customers generate **93.09% of total revenue**
- Top **10% of customers contribute 61.41%** of revenue
- One-time customers generate significantly lower value
- Month-2 retention is only **20.62%**, showing early drop-off

---

## Business Decision

Retention is the highest leverage growth driver.

### Recommended Actions
- Improve post-purchase engagement within 30 days  
- Focus on high-value customer retention  
- Track repeat purchase rate as a core KPI  

---

## Dataset

- UCI Online Retail dataset
