# Customer Retention & Revenue Analysis

> 📊 93.09% of revenue is driven by repeat customers, while the top 10% contribute 61.41% of total sales.

This project analyzes 541K+ real retail transactions to uncover retention risk, revenue concentration, and customer behavior.

## Key Findings

- Repeat customers generate 93.09% of revenue
- Top 10% customers contribute 61.41% of revenue
- Month-2 retention is only 20.62%

## Business Decision

Retention is the highest leverage growth driver.
